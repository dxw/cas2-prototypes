Learning difficulties and Neurodiversity

Neurodiversity covers Autism, ADHD, ADD, Dyslexia, Dyscalculia and Dyspraxia.

This can overlap with learning difficulties. For example, they might find it hard to understand or remember information, or have persistent problems with reading, writing or maths.
