Questions for registered GPs

  1. Do they have any physical health need(s)? Please describe them and the level of severity.
  2. Are they currently receiving any medical treatment for their physical health needs? If so, please describe it.
  3. Are they currently taking any medication for their physical health needs? If so, please provide details.
  4. Can they climb stairs?
  5. Can they live independently?
  6. What support is required?

Questions for Drugs and alcohol teams

The applicant has indicated that they are engaged with support for a drug or alcohol need.

  1. What substance(s) did/do they take?
  2. When do they take it, by what method, and how much?
  3. Are they engaged with a drug and alcohol service? Please name it.
  4. Are they prescribed a substitute medication for their misused substance? Please name it.
