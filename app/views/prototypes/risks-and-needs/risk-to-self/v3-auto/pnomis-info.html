Import data from P-Nomis

ACCT and prison case notes can be imported in to the application from P-Nomis.

P-Nomis alerts help assessors understand behavioural traits and additional needs an applicant may have.

Where P-Nomis alerts are available, a referrer can select the most appropriate and add them to the application.
