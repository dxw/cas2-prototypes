Check eligibility for a CAS-2 placement

CAS-2 accommodation is offered by Nacro, the housing service provider.

Strict criteria must be met in order to apply for CAS-2. Use this service to check whether an applicant is eligible.

Important

To check eligibility, you need the applicant’s CRN number.

Please be aware that eligibility does not guarantee the applicant a CAS-2 placement. You will also need to apply to Nacro on their behalf.

Before you start

Who is CAS-2 for?

CAS-2 provides housing for people who don’t have a suitable address and are:

  • are on Bail Order
  • are on a Home Detention Curfew (HDC) licence
  • who are homeless on conditional release (HCRD)
