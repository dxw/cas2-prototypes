Before you start

Who is CAS-2 for?

CAS-2 provides housing for people who do not have a suitable address and:

  • are on Bail Order
  • are on a Home Detention Curfew (HDC) licence
  • who are homeless on conditional release (HCRD)

To check eligibility you need:

  • the applicant's CRN number
